ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Aqilah, a Japanese Literature student at Universitas Padjadjaran with a deep passion for visual art and illustration. While I study language, culture, and literature academically, my heart truly belongs to the world of drawing and creative expression.

I specialize in both traditional and digital illustration, often inspired by the stories, aesthetics, and symbolism I explore through my studies. I love combining narrative elements with visual storytelling to create art that speaks and connects.

My goal is to grow as an artist, collaborate with other creatives, and eventually build a career in the visual arts industry—whether as an illustrator, concept artist, or visual storyteller.

When I’m not working on my university projects, you’ll usually find me sketching, learning new techniques, or sharing my latest artwork online.
